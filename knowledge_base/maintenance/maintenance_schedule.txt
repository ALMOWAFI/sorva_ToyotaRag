2016 Toyota Sequoia — Maintenance Schedule

Every 5,000 miles or 6 months (whichever comes first):
- Engine oil and oil filter change (5W-30 full synthetic recommended)
- Tire rotation
- Visual inspection of brakes, fluid levels, lights

Every 15,000 miles:
- Cabin air filter inspection and replacement if needed
- Engine air filter inspection
- Brake inspection

Every 30,000 miles:
- Transmission fluid inspection
- Transfer case fluid inspection (4WD models)
- Front and rear differential fluid inspection
- Spark plug inspection

Every 60,000 miles:
- Spark plug replacement (iridium plugs — long life)
- Engine coolant replacement
- Brake fluid replacement
- Fuel filter inspection

Every 90,000 miles:
- Drive belt inspection and replacement if needed
- Transfer case and differential fluid change

Tire Information:
- Recommended tire pressure: 35 PSI front and rear
- Original tire size: 275/65R18
- Spare tire: Full-size matching spare
- Tire rotation pattern: Front-to-back rotation recommended every 5,000 miles

Fluid Specifications:
- Engine oil: 5W-30 (full synthetic preferred)
- Transmission fluid: Toyota ATF Type T-IV or WS
- Coolant: Toyota Super Long Life Coolant (pink)
- Brake fluid: DOT 3
- Power steering fluid: Dexron III ATF
- Rear differential: 75W-90 gear oil (limited slip additive required for limited-slip differential)
- Transfer case: Toyota transfer case fluid or equivalent

Battery:
- Group size: 24F or 27F
- Cold cranking amps: 550 CCA minimum recommended
- Replacement interval: 3-5 years depending on climate
